Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about TECHMA

Are the courses really free?

Yes, all our courses are completely free with no hidden costs. We believe education should be accessible to everyone.

Do I get a certificate after completing a course?

Yes, we provide free digital certificates for all completed courses that you can share on LinkedIn and other platforms.

How long do I have access to the courses?

Once enrolled, you have lifetime access to all course materials, including any future updates.

Can I access courses on mobile devices?

Yes, our platform is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets.

Is there any prerequisite knowledge required?

Most of our beginner courses require no prior knowledge. We clearly indicate any prerequisites for advanced courses.
